About Me

I'm currently a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, under the supervision of Prof. Lei Ying.
Before joining the University of Michigan, I received my Ph.D. degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the
University of British Columbia, where I had the privilege of being advised by Prof. Lele Wang.

My research interest lie broadly in the intersection of information theory, learning theory and graph theory.
